# Phase 3: Authentication & Multi-Role System

We are now moving into Phase 3. This phase focuses on turning the dashboard into a personalized experience.

## Key Goals
1. **User Login**: Citizens can log in to track their reports.
2. **Representative Profiles**: Candidates can claim their profile and respond to citizens.
3. **Verified Badges**: Show who is an official representative.
4. **Member-Only Analytics**: Advanced metrics for logged-in users.
